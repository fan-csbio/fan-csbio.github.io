Brief Biography 

Fang Ge received her M.S. degree from Anhui University and Ph.D. degree from Nanjing University of Science and Technology.She is a member of the Pattern Recognition and Bioinformatics Group. Her current interests include bioinformatics, pattern recognition, machine learning, and data mining.
